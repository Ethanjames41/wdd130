We Help You Book the Right River Day

Every group shows up with different goals. Some want a quick half-day ride with just enough splash to make it memorable. Others want an overnight trip with time to camp, relax, and cover more water. Our guide team can help match your group with the right route and pace.

When you contact us, include your preferred dates, the number of people in your group, and the ages or experience level of your rafters. That gives us enough information to recommend the best option quickly.

What to Ask About

We can explain what each trip feels like, what equipment is included, what to wear, and how far in advance you should reserve. Larger groups and overnight excursions usually need a little more planning, so earlier contact helps.

Before You Reserve

Let us know whether your group wants scenic floating, bigger rapids, or a balance of both. If anyone is nervous about whitewater, mention that too. We would rather recommend the right trip than oversell the wrong one.
