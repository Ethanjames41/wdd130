Adventure That Fits Your Pace

Every trip includes experienced guides, safety gear, and a route matched to the age and comfort level of your group. Whether you want gentle scenery or fast water, we will help you choose the right launch time and river section.

  • Guide-led groups with safety briefing before launch
  • Family-friendly and high-adrenaline options
  • All-day and overnight plans available
  • Simple booking through our guide team

Half-Day Rapids Run

A short, fast trip for visitors who want excitement without committing the whole day. Great for first-time rafters who still want a few memorable splashes.

  • Length: 3 hours
  • Level: Moderate
  • Best for: Teens, couples, small groups

Scenic Family Float

A calmer route with wide views, wildlife spotting, and plenty of time to enjoy the canyon. This is the easiest trip to recommend for mixed-age groups.

  • Length: 4 hours
  • Level: Easy
  • Best for: Families and new rafters

Overnight Canyon Expedition

Spend the day on the river, camp under the stars, and wake up ready for another stretch of water. This option blends rapids, scenery, and campfire time.

  • Length: 2 days
  • Level: Moderate to challenging
  • Best for: Adventure seekers and groups
